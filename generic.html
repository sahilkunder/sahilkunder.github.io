About Me

"I want to help others to make the right data-driven decisions"

Hi, My name is Sahil

I am a data analyst and aerospace engineer passionate about working with organisations that want to make effective use of their data; from the first ideas around what you want to measure and how to capture data accurately, through data preparation, analysis and management, all the way to impactful presentation of insights. I can support each step of the process to enable your organisation in making evidence-driven decisions or share insights with new audiences.
